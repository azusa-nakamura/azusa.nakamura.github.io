About me

Welcome to my website.

I am a 5th year PhD candidate in Business Administration and Management in Bocconi.

My research interests are mechanisms of dynamics of collaboration in creative industry by focusing on social network and status.

My supervisor is Giuseppe Soda. My committe members are Fabrizio Castellucci and Marco Tortoriello. My external reviewers are Tanya Menon and Elisa Operti.

Before joining Bocconi PhD program, I received a Master degree and Bachelor degree from Keio University in Japan. I worked with Hitoshi Mitsuhashi
